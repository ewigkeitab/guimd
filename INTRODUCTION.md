# Introduction to Guimd

**Guimd** is a Markdown editor designed specifically for writing and reading. Simple and lightweight, with no complex features, it offers only a clear and concise interface, allowing you to focus on the most important part.

## Why use Guimd?

### 1. What You See Is What You Get (WYSIWYG)
No more looking at confusing symbols or weird code, nor do you need to memorize Markdown syntax. After copying and pasting, you won't see a bunch of strange symbols.

### 2. Cross-Platform App
Available on macOS, Windows, and Linux. There's no need to look for similar but different editors across different operating systems.

### 3. Lightweight and Low Overhead
Small in size and fast in execution speed. Opening multiple files at the same time won't cause lag, and it doesn't consume system resources.

### 4. Smart Document Outline
Auto-generated, clickable structured index for seamless document navigation via instant visual search targeting.

### 5. Find and Replace
Robust search functionality with match-case options and dynamic visual highlighting across your editor.

### 6. Minimalist Design
Pure focus on your content, complete with an ultra-minimal line-art typewriter app icon.

---

Guimd is lightweight, fast, and does not require a complex installation. Just open it and start writing.
